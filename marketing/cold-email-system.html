Cold Email System SOP

Role: Sales / Setter
When: After mastering cold calling (500+ calls)
Source: Cold Outreach Framework
Last Updated: Feb 22, 2026

Why Cold Email

Cold email is a one-to-many outreach method that:

  • Scales better than calling

  • Works while you sleep

  • Lower rejection (no live "no")

  • Complements cold calling


Prerequisites:
  • 500+ cold calls completed (builds sales intuition)

  • Understand objections from calling

  • Have at least 2-3 testimonials


Email Infrastructure Setup

1. Domain Setup

Never use your main domain for cold email.

Buy 3-5 similar domains:

  • Main: slatesystems.io

  • Cold email: slatesystemsllc.com, slate-systems.co, getslatesystems.com


2. Email Accounts

Create 2-3 email accounts per domain:

  • dan@slatesystemsllc.com

  • support@slatesystemsllc.com

  • team@slatesystemsllc.com


Recommended providers:
  • Google Workspace ($6/month per account)

  • Microsoft 365 ($6/month per account)


3. Warmup Period

New email accounts need 2-3 weeks of warmup before sending.

Tools:
  • Instantly.ai (built-in warmup)
  • Warmbox
  • Lemwarm
During warmup:
  • Send 10-20 emails/day
  • Gradually increase
  • Reply to warmup emails

4. SPF/DKIM/DMARC

Configure these DNS records:

  • SPF: Authorizes sending servers

  • DKIM: Cryptographic signature

  • DMARC: Handling policy


Most email providers give you the records to add.

Finding Prospects

Method 1: Google Maps Scraping

Tools:

  • Outscraper

  • PhantomBuster

  • D7 Lead Finder


Search: "[niche] [city]"
Export: Business name, email, phone, website

Method 2: Apollo.io

  1. Create Apollo account (free tier available)
  2. Filter by:
- Industry (Home Services, HVAC, Plumbing, etc.) - Location (target cities) - Company size (1-10 employees) - No website OR low-quality website

Method 3: LinkedIn + Hunter.io

  1. Find businesses on LinkedIn
  2. Use Hunter.io to find email addresses
  3. Verify emails before sending

Email Verification

Always verify emails before sending.

Tools:

  • NeverBounce

  • ZeroBounce

  • Hunter.io verify


Remove:
  • Invalid emails

  • Catch-all (risky)

  • Role emails (info@, support@)

Sending Strategy

Volume Limits

Per email account per day:
  • Week 1-2: 20-30 emails
  • Week 3-4: 40-50 emails
  • After 1 month: 50-75 emails (max)
Total daily (3 accounts): 150-225 emails

Sending Schedule

  • Send Monday-Friday
  • Best times: 8-10 AM and 1-3 PM (recipient's timezone)
  • Space emails 2-5 minutes apart (not all at once)

Tools

Email sequencing:
  • Instantly.ai (recommended)
  • Lemlist
  • Mailshake
  • Smartlead

CRM Tracking

When prospect replies:

  1. Add to GHL contacts

  2. Tag as "Cold Email Lead"

  3. Note which email they responded to

  4. Move to pipeline


Reply Handling

Positive Reply

"Sure, send me the video"
Response: 
Awesome! Here's a quick video I put together for you: [Loom link]


Let me know what you think. If it makes sense, I can hop on a quick call to walk you through how it works.



What's your schedule like this week?

Interested but Busy

"Not right now, maybe later"
Response: 
Totally understand. When would be a better time to circle back? I'll set a reminder.

Add to 30-day follow-up.

Not Interested

"Not interested"
Response: 
No problem at all. Mind if I ask what you're currently doing for getting new customers? Just curious.

Sometimes opens a conversation.

"How much?"

"What do you charge?"
Response: 
Great question! It depends on what you need, but most of my clients pay around $297-497/month for everything — website, Google optimization, review system, and lead notifications.


Worth a quick call to see if it's a fit? Takes 15 minutes.

Unsubscribe Request

Remove immediately. Never email again.

Common Mistakes

Don't:

  • Send without warming up accounts
  • Use your main domain
  • Send more than 75/day per account
  • Use too many links (1 max)
  • Use spammy words (FREE, GUARANTEED, ACT NOW)
  • Send on weekends
  • Ignore bounces and complaints

Do:

  • Personalize every email
  • Keep it short (under 100 words)
  • Use plain text (no fancy HTML)
  • Test subject lines
  • Track everything
  • Clean your list regularly

A/B Testing

Test one thing at a time:

  1. Subject lines — Run 2 versions, pick winner
  2. Opening line — Test personalization approaches
  3. CTA — "Quick call?" vs "Send video?" vs "Worth chatting?"
  4. Send time — Morning vs afternoon

Compliance

CAN-SPAM Requirements

  • Include physical address
  • Honor unsubscribes within 10 days
  • Accurate "From" name
  • No deceptive subject lines

Best Practice

At bottom of email:

[Your Name]
Slate Systems | [City, State]
Reply STOP to unsubscribe

Daily Workflow

  1. Morning (30 min):
- Check replies from yesterday - Respond to all positives - Log in CRM
  1. Midday:
- Sequences send automatically - Monitor for replies
  1. End of day (15 min):
- Check reply rate - Remove bounces - Note any unsubscribes
