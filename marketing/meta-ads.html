Role: Marketing / Founder
When: After organic methods (cold calling, email) producing consistent results
Source: Kai Stone Systems

When to Start Paid Ads

Prerequisites:
  • 500+ cold calls completed
  • Know your script and objections cold
  • Have 3-5 video testimonials
  • Proven offer (5+ paying clients)
  • Budget: $50-100/day minimum
Why wait:
  • Ads amplify what works (and what doesn't)
  • Need sales skills first
  • Need proof/testimonials for creative
  • Need cash flow to fund ads

Account Setup

1. Business Manager

Create at: business.facebook.com

Structure:
  • Business Manager (company level)
  • Ad Account (billing lives here)
  • Facebook Page (for running ads)
  • Instagram Account (optional)
  • Pixel (tracking)

2. Payment Method

  • Add credit card to Ad Account
  • Set spending limit initially ($500/month to start)

3. Facebook Pixel

Create pixel in Events Manager:

  1. Go to Events Manager

  2. Click "Connect Data Sources"

  3. Select "Web"

  4. Name it "[Business Name] Pixel"

  5. Install on landing page


For GHL pages:
  • Go to Sites ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Tracking Code

  • Add pixel base code to header

  • Add event code for form submissions

Targeting

Location

  • Target 25-50 mile radius around service area
  • Or target specific ZIP codes
  • Exclude areas you don't serve

Demographics

Home services (plumber, HVAC, roofer):
  • Age: 30-65
  • Homeowners (select in detailed targeting)
  • Income: Middle to upper
B2B services:
  • Age: 25-55
  • Small business owners
  • Interested in: Business, Entrepreneurship

Interests (Home Services Example)

  • Home improvement
  • Home & Garden
  • DIY
  • Recently moved
  • Homeowners
  • Property management

Audience Size

  • Local market: 50,000 - 500,000
  • Too small = expensive, limited reach
  • Too large = less targeted

Creative Best Practices

Video Ads (Best Performing)

Format:
  • Vertical (9:16) for Stories/Reels
  • Square (1:1) for Feed
  • Length: 15-60 seconds
Structure:
  1. Hook (0-3 sec): Stop the scroll
- "If you're a homeowner in [City]..." - "I just saved this family $2,000..." - Show dramatic before/after
  1. Problem (3-10 sec): Agitate the pain
- "Are you tired of..." - "Most plumbers will..."
  1. Solution (10-25 sec): Your offer
- What you do differently - Quick testimonial clip - Show results
  1. CTA (last 5 sec):
- "Click below to get a free quote" - "Send us a message" - "Book your free inspection"

Image Ads

What works:
  • Before/after transformations
  • Team photos (real people)
  • 5-star review screenshots
  • Simple, clean graphics
Avoid:
  • Stock photos
  • Too much text (under 20% rule)
  • Cluttered designs

Landing Pages

Must-Have Elements

  1. Headline ‚Äî Clear benefit
  2. Trust badges ‚Äî Google rating, BBB, etc.
  3. Form ‚Äî Name, phone, service needed
  4. Social proof ‚Äî Reviews, testimonials
  5. CTA button ‚Äî "Get Free Quote"
  6. Contact info ‚Äî Phone, address

Form Fields

Keep it minimal:

  • Name

  • Phone number

  • Service needed (dropdown)

  • Address (optional)


More fields = fewer leads (but higher quality)

Mobile Optimization

80%+ of traffic is mobile. Check:

  • Fast load time (<3 seconds)

  • Tap targets large enough

  • Form fills easily on mobile

  • Click-to-call button works


Budget & Bidding

Starting Budget

  • Testing phase: $20-50/day
  • Scaling phase: $50-200/day
  • Aggressive: $200+/day

Bid Strategy

Start with:
  • Lowest cost (let Facebook optimize)
  • Conversions optimization (not link clicks)
Advanced:
  • Cost cap (set max cost per lead)
  • Bid cap (set max bid)

Budget Allocation

  • 70% to best-performing ad set
  • 30% to testing new creative

Campaign Types

1. Lead Generation (Recommended to Start)

Objective: Leads Optimization: Conversions Conversion Event: Form submission on landing page Flow: Ad ‚Üí Landing page ‚Üí Form ‚Üí Thank you page ‚Üí Lead in GHL

2. Messages Campaign

Objective: Messages Destination: Facebook Messenger or Instagram DM Best for:
  • Quick response businesses
  • Simple services
  • When you can respond within 5 minutes

3. Retargeting

Audience: People who visited your site but didn't convert Setup:
  1. Create Custom Audience from pixel
  2. Include: Website visitors last 30 days
  3. Exclude: People who already converted
Creative: "Still thinking about [service]? Here's 10% off this week."

Testing Framework

Phase 1: Creative Testing (Week 1-2)

Run 3-5 different creatives to same audience:

  • Different hooks

  • Video vs image

  • Different testimonials


Winner: Highest CTR + lowest CPL

Phase 2: Audience Testing (Week 2-3)

Test winning creative against different audiences:

  • Different age ranges

  • Different interests

  • Different locations


Phase 3: Copy Testing (Week 3-4)

Test different headlines and primary text with winning creative + audience.

Phase 4: Scale (Week 4+)

Increase budget 20-30% every 3 days on winning combinations.

Key Metrics

Monitor Daily

MetricGoodAction if Bad
CPM<$15Creative fatigue, change ad
CTR>1%Hook not working
CPL<$50*Improve targeting or offer
Lead quality>50% qualifiedTighten targeting
*CPL varies by industry. Home services: $20-75/lead typical.

Weekly Review

  • Which ad is winning?
  • Which audience is cheapest?
  • Are leads converting to calls?
  • Are calls converting to sales?

Troubleshooting

High CPM (>$25)

  • Audience too small
  • Creative is getting flagged
  • Competition is high
  • Refresh creative

Low CTR (<0.5%)

  • Hook isn't grabbing attention
  • Creative doesn't stand out
  • Audience mismatch
  • Try video instead of image

High CPL (>$100)

  • Landing page not converting
  • Wrong audience
  • Offer not compelling
  • Form too long

Leads Not Answering

  • Call within 5 minutes
  • Try text first
  • Lead quality issue (refine targeting)

Ad Account Health

Avoid Getting Banned

  • Don't make false claims
  • Don't guarantee results
  • No before/after that looks "too good"
  • Follow Facebook's ad policies
  • Use a real business page (not personal)

If Restricted

  1. Check what policy was violated
  2. Appeal if unjust
  3. Fix the issue
  4. Request review

Daily Routine

Morning (15 min):
  • Check overnight leads
  • Note CPL trend
  • Check for any ad issues
Midday:
  • Respond to leads immediately
  • Check message campaigns
End of day (15 min):
  • Review daily spend
  • Note winning/losing ads
  • Plan adjustments

Scaling Checklist

Before scaling budget:

  • CPL is profitable (lead cost < 1/3 of LTV)
  • Lead quality is good (50%+ qualified)
  • Can handle more leads (sales capacity)
  • Creative isn't fatigued (frequency <3)
  • Landing page converts well (>20%)
