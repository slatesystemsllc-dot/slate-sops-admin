CREATIVE STRATEGY

What Works for Contractors

1. UGC Talking Head (Best)
  • Real person on camera
  • Speaks directly to their pain
  • 15-30 seconds
  • Vertical (9:16)
2. Before/After Results
  • "Client went from X to Y leads/month"
  • Screenshots of notifications
  • Real numbers
3. Testimonial Clips
  • Client speaking about results
  • 30 seconds max
  • Caption: "Contractor gets 40 leads/month"
4. Problem/Agitate/Solution
  • Hook: Call out the pain
  • Agitate: "How many leads did you lose this week?"
  • Solution: "We solve this in 10 days"

BUDGET & SCALING

Phase 1: Testing ($50/day)

  • Test 3-5 creatives
  • Same audience
  • Run 7 days
  • Pick winner by CPL

Phase 2: Audience Testing ($75/day)

  • Winning creative
  • Test different interests
  • Test different locations
  • Run 7 days

Phase 3: Scaling ($100-200/day)

  • Winning creative + audience
  • Increase budget 20% every 3 days
  • Monitor frequency (<3)
  • Add new creative before fatigue

Budget Rules

  • Never increase more than 20% at once
  • Watch frequency â€” over 3 means fatigue
  • Keep 70% on winners, 30% on testing
  • Pause losers after 3 days if CPL >2x target

TROUBLESHOOTING

High CPL (>$75)

  1. Check landing page conversion rate
  2. Is hook relevant to audience?
  3. Is offer clear in first 3 seconds?
  4. Try different creative angle
  5. Narrow audience (too broad?)

Low CTR (<0.5%)

  1. Hook isn't stopping scroll
  2. Thumbnail not compelling
  3. Wrong audience targeting
  4. Try video instead of image
  5. Test different first frame

Leads Not Answering

  1. Call within 5 minutes (WF handles this)
  2. Check if phone is real (qualification issue)
  3. SMS before calling
  4. Leave voicemail + text follow-up
  5. See show-rate-optimization.md

Account Restricted

  1. Check what policy violated
  2. Appeal if legitimate
  3. Common issues:
- "Before/after" too dramatic - Income claims without proof - Misleading button text
  1. Fix and request review
