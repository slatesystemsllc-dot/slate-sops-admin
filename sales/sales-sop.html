1. PRE-CALL: VSL REQUIREMENT

Why the VSL is Non-Negotiable

The VSL does 90% of the selling:

  • Explains what we do

  • Shows real results

  • States the price ($297)

  • Pre-handles objections


Without VSL: 30-45 min call, low close rate, exhausting
With VSL: 5-10 min call, high close rate, scalable

VSL Delivery Sequence

[Lead fills form]
    ↓
[Immediate text]: "Hey [Name]! Before our call, watch this
5-min video so you know exactly what we do: [VSL LINK]"
    ↓
[24hr before call text]: "Hey [Name], just a reminder about
our call tomorrow at [TIME]. Make sure you've watched this
quick video first: [VSL LINK]"
    ↓
[2hr before call text]: "See you in 2 hours! Quick reminder
to watch this if you haven't: [VSL LINK]"

2. THE TIGHT SCRIPT

Full Script with Timing

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
PHASE 1: CONFIRM VIDEO (15 seconds)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════


YOU: "Hey [Name], how's it going man?"



[Let them respond]



YOU: "Awesome. Hey quick question - did you get a chance to
watch that short video I sent over?"



→ IF YES: Go to Phase 2
→ IF NO: Go to Section 3 (Handling "I Haven't Watched")



═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
PHASE 2: OPEN FOR QUESTIONS (10 seconds)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════



YOU: "Perfect, you're the man. So yeah, that video is pretty
much exactly what we do. That being said - what questions
do you have?"



→ IF QUESTIONS: Answer briefly, then ask "What other questions?"
→ IF NO QUESTIONS: Go to Phase 4 (Permission to Close)



═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
PHASE 3: ANSWER LOOP (1-5 minutes)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════



[Answer their question - keep it brief, 30-60 seconds max]



YOU: "Does that make sense?"



[They confirm]



YOU: "Cool. What other questions do you have?"



[Repeat until they say "no more questions" or "I think I'm good"]



═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
PHASE 4: PERMISSION TO CLOSE (10 seconds)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════



YOU: "Awesome. Any other questions before I get you going?"



→ IF "NO": Go to Phase 5
→ IF MORE QUESTIONS: Answer, repeat Phase 4
→ IF OBJECTION: Handle it (see Section 4), then repeat Phase 4



═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
PHASE 5: CLOSE (1-2 minutes)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════



YOU: "Perfect. Let me grab some info from you real quick."



"What's the official business name?"
[Write it down]



"And what's the best address for the business?"
[Write it down]



"Cool. And what's the card number?"
[Enter into Stripe/GHL]



"Expiration?"
[Enter]



"And the 3 digits on the back?"
[Enter]



"Perfect, you're all set. You're gonna get a text from us
in about 20 minutes with the onboarding form. Fill that
out and we'll have everything built in about 8-9 days."

3. HANDLING "I HAVEN'T WATCHED"

Scenario A: They're Ready to Watch Now

THEM: "No, I didn't get a chance to watch it yet."


YOU: "No worries at all. It's only about 5 minutes.
You have a sec to watch it real quick? I can wait."



THEM: "Yeah sure, let me pull it up."



YOU: "Perfect. I just sent it to you again via text.
Let me know when you've got it pulled up."



[Wait for them to confirm]



YOU: "Cool, I'll just hang here. Take your time, watch
the whole thing, and let me know when you're done."



[MUTE YOURSELF. Let them watch. 5-6 minutes.]



[When they finish]



YOU: "Cool, you're the man for watching that. So that's
pretty much exactly what we do. What questions do you have?"



[Continue with Phase 2]

Scenario B: They're Driving

THEM: "No, I'm actually driving right now."


YOU: "No worries man, safety first. Here's what we can do -
I'll shoot you the link right now, you watch it when you
get where you're going, and then just text me 'ready' and
I'll call you right back. Sound good?"



THEM: "Yeah that works."



YOU: "Perfect. What's the best number to text you on -
is it this one you're calling from?"



[Confirm number]



YOU: "Cool. Watch the video, text me 'ready', and I'll
call you within 5 minutes. Talk soon."



[End call. Send VSL link immediately. Wait for their text.]

Scenario C: They're Busy / In a Rush

THEM: "No, I didn't watch it. Can you just give me the
quick rundown?"


YOU: "I hear you man, but honestly the video does a way
better job than I can in 2 minutes. It shows you real
client results and exactly how everything works.



Tell you what - it's only 5 minutes. Watch it tonight
and let's reschedule for tomorrow. What time works?"



[Book new appointment]



YOU: "Perfect. I'll send you the link right now. Watch
it before our call tomorrow and come with any questions.
Talk then."

Scenario D: They Refuse to Watch

THEM: "Can't you just tell me what it is? I don't have
time for videos."


YOU: "I totally get it. Here's the thing though - we've
found that guys who watch the video first have way better
calls because we're on the same page. Otherwise I'm just
gonna be repeating everything that's in the video anyway.



It's 5 minutes. Can you commit to watching it before we
talk again?"



→ IF YES: Reschedule
→ IF STILL NO: "No worries man, sounds like the timing
   might not be right. Feel free to book another call
   when you've got a few minutes to check it out."



[End call. Don't waste time on people who won't invest
5 minutes. They won't be good clients.]

Key Principles

  1. Never do the demo call without them watching the VSL
  2. It's OK to wait on the line while they watch
  3. It's OK to reschedule
  4. It's OK to end the call if they refuse
  5. Driving = reschedule or callback, never do the call

4. OBJECTION HANDLING

"How many leads will I get?"

"I'm gonna be straight with you - I can't tell you that
Susan is gonna see your Google reviews and give you a
$20K job. I can't prove that correlation.


What I do know is the contractors who have professional
websites, tons of reviews, and fast follow-up are the
ones winning. We give you all the systems to make that
happen.



You think it's worth $297 or you don't. But I'm not
gonna promise you X leads per month because that would
be bullshit."

"I've been burned by other companies"

"I hear that a lot actually. Most marketing companies
overpromise and underdeliver.


Here's how we're different - I'm not promising you leads.
I'm giving you systems. The website, the reviews, the
missed call follow-up. You saw it in the video.



It either makes sense for $297 or it doesn't. No contracts,
cancel anytime. What other questions do you have?"

"That's expensive" / "I need to think about it"

"I get it. Let me ask you this - what's your average job
ticket? $5K? $10K?


So if these systems help you land ONE extra job this year,
you're 3-4x'ing what you paid us. One job.



And honestly, $297 is about what you'd spend taking your
crew to lunch twice. But I hear you - what specifically
do you need to think about?"

"Do you have contracts?"

"Nope. No contracts. Those are just used to trap people
who want to leave. We don't do that.


You can cancel anytime. If we're not delivering value,
you shouldn't be paying us. Simple as that."

"Let me talk to my wife/partner"

"Totally get it. When do you think you'll have that
conversation?


[They give timeframe]



Cool, let's get something on the calendar for [day after].
That way I can answer any questions they have too.
What time works?"

"I don't have a website yet"

"That's actually perfect. Means we don't have to convince
you your current website sucks. We just build you a good
one from scratch.


What other questions do you have?"

"I don't have a Google Business page"

"That's free to set up. Takes about 5 days to get verified.
I'll send you a video showing exactly how to do it.


While you're getting that verified, we'll build your
website. By the time it's approved, everything will be
ready to connect.



What other questions?"

5. CLOSING SEQUENCE

Order Matters: Card LAST

  1. Business name
  2. Address
  3. Email (if needed)
  4. Card number
  5. Expiration
  6. CVV
Why this order: By the time you ask for the card, they've already given you 3-4 pieces of info. They're committed. The card feels like "one more thing" not "the big moment."

If Card Declines

YOU: "Hmm, looks like that one didn't go through.
You have another card we can try?"


[If yes, try again]



[If no]



YOU: "No worries. Sometimes it's a daily limit thing.
Want to try again tomorrow? I can call you same time."

If They Hesitate at Card

THEM: [Long pause when you ask for card]


YOU: "You good? Any other questions before we get
you set up?"



[Let them voice the concern, then handle it]

6. POST-CALL: HANDOFF

Immediately After Close

  1. Add to GHL pipeline - Move to "Onboarding Form Sent" stage
  2. Send onboarding text:
   "Hey [Name]! You're all set. Here's your onboarding form:
   [LINK]. Fill this out so we can build your website.
   Any questions, just text me here."
  1. Send onboarding email - Same link, backup
  2. Notify fulfillment - Slack/message to whoever builds

Onboarding Form Must Collect

  • Business name
  • Owner name
  • Phone
  • Address
  • Services offered (list)
  • Service areas (list)
  • Logo (upload)
  • Photos (upload)
  • Website URL (if existing)
  • Google Business Profile URL
  • Any special notes

Timeline Expectation

Tell customer: "8-9 business days" to complete build
