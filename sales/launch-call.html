Pre-Call Prep (Before Client Joins)

Verify in GHL:

  • Website live and loading
  • All automations configured
  • Phone number provisioned (or status known)
  • Review funnel tested
  • Chat widget working
  • Quote form tested
  • Client's sub-account credentials ready

Know These Blockers:

  • A2P status (pending = explain 5-7 day wait)
  • GMB status (connected or needs client action)
  • Missing assets (photos, social links)

1. Open (2 min)

"Everything's done, just want to show you what we built"
  • Address blockers upfront if any
  • Set expectation: 30 min walkthrough

2. App Setup (5 min)

Check:

  • Lead Connector app downloaded?
  • If not, guide them: iPhone / Android links

If login issues:

  • Reset password (must include special character)
  • Show inbox view
Key message: "You'll never have to use the app besides seeing conversations"

3. Website Walkthrough (10 min)

Walk through in this order:

  • Homepage + quote form
  • Chat widget (test it live)
  • Service pages (explain SEO value)
  • Service area pages (explain SEO value)
  • Contact page
  • Reviews section (auto-populates from Google)
  • About Us (collect photo if missing)
  • Privacy policy + terms (compliance)

4. Review System Demo (10 min)

This is their favorite feature. Spend time here.
  • Show 5-star funnel live
  • Explain: 4-5 stars ‚Üí Google, 1-3 stars ‚Üí private feedback
  • Show the marketing form in app
  • Demo: enter a test customer
"This is literally the only thing you'll ever have to do"

Explain the automation:

  • 5 reminders over 4 weeks
  • 1-year follow-up sequence
  • Limit: 70-80 people/day max

5. Auto Call Text-Back Demo (5 min)

  • Test phone number forwarding (call their toll-free)
  • Show missed call workflow
  • Explain misspelling trick (seems human)

6. Dream Outcome (5 min)

Show example results:

  • 300+ reviews
  • 800+ customer interactions
  • Zero paid ads for organic traffic
"At 6-8 months: 70+ reviews, Google becomes your organic acquisition system"
Key message: "These are high-intent leads coming directly to you, not being sold to 10 people at once"

7. Wrap-up (5 min)

Send via SMS during call:

  • Website URL
  • Their quick links page: [their-site]/my-links
  • Help center: help.slatesystems.io
"Bookmark your quick links page ‚Äî that's where you'll find your website URL, review link, and phone number anytime you need them. And if you ever forget how something works, help.slatesystems.io has guides for everything."

Collect if missing:

  • About Us photo
  • Social media links

Offer:

  • Business cards (~$20)
"Check back in 2-3 months for SEO discussion"

Post-Call Actions

In GHL:

  • Move opportunity to "Active" stage
  • Verify currentclient tag added (WF-OB6 does this)

Send within 24 hours:

  • Post-launch Loom video (see Post-Launch Video SOP)
  • Reference help.slatesystems.io in video
  • Reference their /my-links page for quick access

In Slack/Notes:

  • Log any issues or follow-ups needed
  • Note if testimonial/referral was requested

Common Blockers + Scripts

A2P Registration Pending

"Your phone number is in the verification queue. Takes 5-7 business days. You can still receive calls, but automated texts won't go out until approved. I'll let you know as soon as it's live."

A2P Rejected

"The carrier rejected the first submission. We're resubmitting with corrections. I'll push back your billing until it's resolved."

GMB Not Verified

"Google needs to verify your business. You'll get a postcard in 5-7 days with a code. As soon as you get it, text me the code and I'll enter it."

GMB Claimed by Someone Else

"Looks like someone else claimed your Google listing. We need to request access or recover it. I'll send you the steps."

Missing About Us Photo

"Can you send me a photo of yourself or your team? Doesn't have to be professional - just something real. Makes a big difference for trust."
Billing Adjustment Rule: "If we promised 14 days and it's not done, push back billing by however many days it takes." Document any billing adjustments in client notes.
