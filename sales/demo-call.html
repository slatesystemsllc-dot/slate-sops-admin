Before the Call

1. Confirm VSL Was Sent

  • Initial VSL text (after booking)
  • 24hr reminder with VSL link
  • 2hr reminder with VSL link

2. Quick Research (2 min)

  • Search "[business name] [city]" ‚Üí their Google Business Profile
  • Check their website (if any)
  • Note: review count, website quality, obvious issues
What to look for:
‚Ä¢ No website = "best case scenario"
‚Ä¢ Bad website = point out issues
‚Ä¢ Few reviews = "8 reviews after 18 years?"
‚Ä¢ Phone not clickable = accessibility issue

The Script (Tight)

Phase 1: Video Check (15 sec)

"Hey [Name], how's it going?" [Let them respond] "Quick question - did you watch that short video I sent over?" ‚Üí YES: Go to Phase 2 ‚Üí NO: Go to "Didn't Watch" section

Phase 2: Open for Questions (10 sec)

"Perfect, appreciate you watching. That video is pretty much exactly what we do." "That being said - what questions do you have?"

Phase 3: Answer Loop (1-5 min)

1. Answer question (30-60 sec max) 2. "Does that make sense?" 3. "What OTHER questions do you have?" THE HAMMER (use when they ramble): "Cool. What other questions do you have?"

Phase 4: Permission to Close

"Awesome. Any other questions before I get you going?" ‚Üí "No": CLOSE ‚Üí More questions: Answer, repeat ‚Üí Objection: Handle it, repeat

Phase 5: Close (1-2 min)

"Perfect. Let me grab some info." "What's the official business name?" "And what's the best address?" "Cool. And what's the card number?" "Expiration?" "3 digits on the back?" "Perfect, you're all set. You'll get a text in 20 minutes with the onboarding form. 8-9 days to build."
CRITICAL: Card number is ALWAYS last.

They Didn't Watch the Video

Option A: Watch Now

"No worries. It's only 5 minutes. Have a sec to watch it? I can wait." [If yes - MUTE yourself, wait 5-6 min] "Cool, what questions do you have?"

Option B: Driving

"Safety first. I'll send the link now, you watch when you get there, text me 'ready' and I'll call right back."

Option C: Too Busy

"The video does a way better job than I can in 2 min. Watch it tonight, let's reschedule for tomorrow. What time?"

Option D: Refuses

"We've found guys who watch first have better calls. Can you commit to watching before we talk again?" [If still no] "Sounds like timing might not be right. Book another call when you've got a few minutes."

Objection Handling

"How many leads will I get?"

"I can't tell you Susan sees your reviews and gives you $20K. What I know is contractors with professional websites, reviews, and fast follow-up are winning. We give you the systems. You think it's worth $297 or you don't."

"I've been burned before"

"Most marketing companies overpromise. I'm not promising leads. I'm giving you systems. Website, reviews, missed call follow-up. $297, no contracts. What other questions?"

"That's expensive"

"What's your average job? $5K? $10K? ONE extra job this year = 3-4x what you paid us. $297 is crew lunch twice."

"Let me talk to my wife"

"Totally get it. When will you have that conversation? Cool, let's get something on the calendar for [day after]. I can answer their questions too."

"I don't have a website"

"That's actually perfect. We don't have to convince you your current website sucks. We just build you a good one."

üí° Lightbulb Moments

Use when prospect needs convincing:

The Phone Number Test

"Pull up your website. See how your phone number isn't clickable? My mom's in her 70s, bad eyes - she can't read that small text. She's gonna leave and call the next guy."

The Review Trap

"You have 8 reviews after 18 years. I KNOW you've served more than 8 people. You just don't ask. We automate that - every job done, review request goes out. They get reminded 4 times."

The Missed Call Reality

"When you're on a roof and miss a call, what happens? They call the next guy. With auto text back, they get 'Hey sorry, with a customer, call you back in 20 min?' and they wait for YOU."

The Susan Story

"Susan is shopping for a $50K kitchen remodel. She Googles you - 8 reviews, janky website. She Googles your competitor - 150 reviews, beautiful site. Who's she calling?"
