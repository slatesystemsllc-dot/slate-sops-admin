Why This Matters

Math: If you book 20 calls/week:

Before fixing anything else in sales, fix show rate.

3 Fast Wins (Do These First)

1. Tighten Booking Window

Problem: Prospects who book a week out forget, lose interest.

Fix: Cap scheduling to 48-72 hours maximum.

  • Set "Days in advance" to 2-3 days max
  • Remove availability beyond 72 hours
  • The closer to now, the higher the show

2. Call Every Appointment

When they book:

  1. Call within 5 minutes
  2. Confirm the time
  3. Try to pull meeting forward
  4. Build rapport (they show for people they've talked to)

Morning of:

  1. Call to confirm
  2. "Hey, just confirming we're still on for [time]"
  3. If voicemail, text them
This single habit dramatically increases shows.

3. Upgrade Thank You Page

After they book, add:

  1. Short video (30-60 sec) - "Here's what we'll cover..."
  2. Big "Add to Calendar" button - Google + iCal
  3. Commitment checkbox - "Is there any reason you wouldn't show?"

All Levers

Lever 1: Expand Calendar Availability

  • Minimum 6-8 slots per day
  • Shorten duration if needed (45 min ‚Üí 30 min)
  • Offer varied times: early morning, lunch, evening

Lever 2: Multi-Touch Confirmation

Calls:

  1. Immediately after booking
  2. 60 minutes later if no answer
  3. 24 hours before
  4. Morning of
  5. 1 hour before
  6. 10 minutes before

Texts: Confirmation, 24hr reminder, 1hr reminder with link

Lever 3: Reframe Call Value

Don't say: "Demo", "Quick chat", "Let's talk"

Do say: "Strategy session", "I'll Google your business and show quick wins"

Lever 4: Speed to Lead

  • Call leads within 30 seconds
  • 4,000% increase vs waiting 5 minutes
  • First to call usually wins

Lever 5: Double Booking

When you have excess leads:

  1. Book 2 people in same slot
  2. Call both 10-15 min before
  3. Most ready ‚Üí video call
  4. Less ready ‚Üí close on phone or rebook

New Appointment Flow

When someone books:

  • Call within 5 minutes
  • Try to pull meeting forward
  • If no answer, text confirmation
  • Ensure they're in reminder sequence
  • Check if VSL watched (send reminder if not)

Day of:

  • Morning call to confirm
  • 1-hour reminder (call or text)
  • 10-minute reminder with link
  • If no show in 5 min, call them
