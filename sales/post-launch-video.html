Post-Launch Video SOP

Role: Closer (or Founder during absorption)
When: Within 24 hours of launch call completion
Time: ~15-20 minutes to record and send

Purpose

Create a personalized Loom video walking the client through their new system. Reinforces value, reduces support questions, and increases client retention.

When to Send

TriggerTimingChannel
Launch call completedWithin 24 hoursSMS + Email
Client marked ActiveSame day if possibleAutomate via workflow

Video Structure (10-12 minutes)

Intro (30 sec)

"Hey [First Name]! It's [Your Name] from Slate Systems. I wanted to send you a quick walkthrough of your new website and tools so you have everything you need to hit the ground running."

Section 1: The Website (3 min)

Show their actual live website:

  1. Homepage Tour
- Header with their logo - Phone number (clickable on mobile) - Hero section with their image - Services overview - Quote form
  1. Key Pages
- Service pages (click one example) - About page - Service area pages - Contact page
  1. Mobile View
- Show responsive design - Click-to-call works - Chat widget position

Section 2: The App (3 min)

Open the mobile app or dashboard:

  1. Login/Access
- Show how to log in - Quick tour of dashboard
  1. Leads & Contacts
- Where new leads appear - How to see contact details - How to respond to leads
  1. Conversations
- SMS inbox - How to reply - All channels in one place
  1. Notifications
- Enable push notifications - What notifications look like - Importance of fast response

Section 3: Getting Reviews (2 min)

  1. Their Review Link
- Show/send the GMB review link - "Send this to every happy customer"
  1. How It Works
- Customer clicks link - Rates experience - 5-star → goes to Google - Lower → private feedback
  1. Best Practices
- Ask right after job completion - Text the link, don't just mention it - "The more 5-star reviews, the more leads"

Section 4: Chat Widget (1 min)

  1. Where It Appears
- Point out widget on site - Demo sending a message
  1. Where Messages Go
- Show in app/dashboard - Real-time notifications

Section 5: Quick Tips (1 min)

  1. Response Time
- "Respond to leads within 5 minutes if possible" - "Speed to lead = winning the job"
  1. Keep Your Info Updated
- Contact us if hours/services change - We can add before/after photos anytime
  1. Your Resources
- "Bookmark your quick links page: yoursite.com/my-links" - "That's where your website URL, review link, and phone number live" - "If you ever forget how something works: help.slatesystems.io"

Closing (30 sec)

"That's everything! Remember — your quick links are at yoursite.com/my-links, and if you ever need a refresher on anything, help.slatesystems.io has guides for everything. Questions? Just text me. Talk soon, [First Name]!"

Recording Tips

Equipment

  • Loom (free or Pro): loom.com
  • Quiet environment
  • Good lighting if on camera (optional)

Screen Setup

  1. Open client website in one tab
  2. Open app/dashboard in another tab
  3. Have review link copied
  4. Close unrelated tabs

Recording Settings

  • Screen + Camera bubble (optional but personal)
  • 720p minimum
  • Draw/highlight enabled
  • Don't pause/restart excessively - natural is better

Quality Checks

  • Audio clear? No background noise?
  • Screen visible? Text readable?
  • Under 15 minutes? (shorter = better)
  • Personalized with their name/business?

Loom Best Practices

Title Format

[Business Name] - Slate Systems Walkthrough

Thumbnail

  • Use first frame or screenshot of their website
  • Makes it recognizable

Sharing Settings

  • Anyone with link can view
  • Download disabled
  • Comments enabled (optional)

Organization

Create a Loom folder: "Client Walkthroughs"
  • Easy to find later if client asks
  • Track view counts

Common Mistakes to Avoid

MistakeFix
Too long (20+ min)Stay under 12 minutes
Generic scriptUse their name/business throughout
Technical jargon"Dashboard" not "CRM", "Inbox" not "Pipeline"
RushingPause between sections, speak clearly
Wrong websiteDouble-check URL before recording
Notifications on screenEnable DND before recording
Wrong appUse the white-label app if applicable

Automation (Future)

Trigger: Opportunity moved to "Active"

Workflow:
  1. Wait 4 hours (after launch call)
  2. Send SMS: "Video walkthrough coming shortly!"
  3. Create task for Closer to record
  4. Task reminder at 24 hours if not completed

  • Review funnel explanation

Personalize:
  • Intro/outro with their name
