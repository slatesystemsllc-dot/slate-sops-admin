Purpose

Create a personalized Loom video walking the client through their new system. Reinforces value, reduces support questions, and increases client retention.

Send within 24 hours of launch call completion.

When to Send

TriggerTimingChannel
Launch call completedWithin 24 hoursSMS + Email
Client marked ActiveSame day if possibleAutomate via workflow

Video Structure (10-12 minutes)

Intro (30 sec)

"Hey [First Name]! It's [Your Name] from Slate Systems. I wanted to send you a quick walkthrough of your new website and tools so you have everything you need to hit the ground running."

Section 1: The Website (3 min)

Show their actual live website:

  1. Homepage Tour - Header, logo, phone number, hero section, services, quote form
  2. Key Pages - Service pages, About page, Service area pages, Contact
  3. Mobile View - Show responsive design, click-to-call, chat widget

Section 2: The App (3 min)

  1. Login/Access - Show how to log in, quick tour of dashboard
  2. Leads & Contacts - Where new leads appear, how to respond
  3. Conversations - SMS inbox, how to reply
  4. Notifications - Enable push notifications, importance of fast response

Section 3: Getting Reviews (2 min)

  1. Their Review Link - "Send this to every happy customer"
  2. How It Works - 5-star ‚Üí Google, lower ‚Üí private feedback
  3. Best Practices - Ask right after job, text the link

Section 4: Chat Widget (1 min)

  1. Where it appears on site
  2. Demo sending a message
  3. Where messages go in app/dashboard

Section 5: Quick Tips (1 min)

  1. Response Time - "Respond within 5 minutes if possible"
  2. Keep Info Updated - Contact us if hours/services change
  3. Resources - Bookmark yoursite.com/my-links, help.slatesystems.io

Closing (30 sec)

"That's everything! Remember ‚Äî your quick links are at yoursite.com/my-links, and if you ever need a refresher, help.slatesystems.io has guides for everything. Questions? Just text me. Talk soon, [First Name]!"

Recording Tips

Equipment

  • Loom (free or Pro): loom.com
  • Quiet environment
  • Good lighting if on camera (optional)

Screen Setup

  1. Open client website in one tab
  2. Open app/dashboard in another tab
  3. Have review link copied
  4. Close unrelated tabs

Recording Settings

  • Screen + Camera bubble (optional but personal)
  • 720p minimum
  • Draw/highlight enabled
  • Don't restart excessively - natural is better

Quality Checks

  • Audio clear? No background noise?
  • Screen visible? Text readable?
  • Under 15 minutes?
  • Personalized with their name/business?
