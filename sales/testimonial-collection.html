Why This Matters

Testimonials are your most powerful sales asset:

  • Social proof converts skeptical leads
  • Video testimonials outperform text 10x
  • Your first 15-30 clients are your BEST opportunity
  • Once you have team/CSMs, the relationship weakens
Critical Window: Collect testimonials while YOU are still talking to clients directly.

Best Timing (When to Ask)

Ask when they're at their absolute happiest:

MomentWhy It Works
Right after showing websiteThey're excited seeing their new site
When they get first leadThey just saw tangible value
When they text something positiveAlready expressing satisfaction
After a great review comes inSeeing the review system work
Don't wait. Happiness is fleeting. Capture it immediately.

The Process

Step 1: Recognize the Moment

Watch for signals:

  • "This looks amazing!"
  • "Wow, I love it"
  • "I just got a lead from the website!"
  • "This is exactly what I needed"
  • Any unsolicited positive feedback

Step 2: Stop Everything

When you hear/read something positive:

  1. Stop whatever you're doing
  2. Call them immediately (don't text)
  3. Don't let more than 30 minutes pass

Step 3: Chat First, Then Ask

Don't lead with the ask. Build on the positive energy.

"Hey [Name]! I saw your message ‚Äî that's awesome! Tell me more about the lead you got." [Let them talk, share excitement] "Man, I love hearing that. This is exactly why I do this."

Step 4: Make the Ask

"Hey, quick favor ‚Äî would you mind recording a quick 30-second video about your experience? Just talk about what it was like working with us. It would mean a lot and really help other contractors see what we do."

If they hesitate:

"It doesn't have to be anything fancy ‚Äî just your phone, couple sentences. I'll even send you some questions to answer if that helps."

Step 5: Get It NOW

Critical: Don't let them say "I'll do it later." Later = never.
"Do you have 2 minutes right now? We could even do it on this call if you want."

If truly can't do now:

"No worries ‚Äî when's a good time today I can call you back for it?"

Schedule a specific time, don't leave it open.

Backup: The $100 Incentive

If they're hesitant or "too busy":

"Tell you what ‚Äî if you record a quick video testimonial for us, I'll credit $100 to your next month. Just as a thank you."
Use sparingly. Best testimonials come from genuine excitement, not payment.

Questions to Give Them

If they want guidance, send these:

  1. What was your situation before working with us?
  2. What made you decide to sign up?
  3. What's been the biggest benefit so far?
  4. Would you recommend us to other contractors?
"Just answer any 2-3 of these in your own words. 30-60 seconds is perfect."

Video Requirements

  • Selfie-style on phone is fine
  • Horizontal preferred, vertical okay
  • Good lighting (face a window)
  • Quiet environment
  • 30-90 seconds ideal
Don't ask for perfection. Authentic > polished.

How to Receive Videos

  1. Text it to you ‚Äî Easiest for them
  2. Email attachment ‚Äî For larger files
  3. Loom ‚Äî If they're comfortable
  4. Record during Zoom call ‚Äî You record, get permission
