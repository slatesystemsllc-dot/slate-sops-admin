Two Registration Paths

Path 1: A2P 10DLC (Standard)

  • Best for: Clients with 45+ day old EIN
  • Cost: $23.95-$68.05 one-time + $12/mo
  • Approval time: 2-4 business days
  • Message throughput: Higher limits

Path 2: Toll-Free Verification

  • Best for: New businesses, clients without old EIN
  • Cost: FREE
  • Approval time: 1-2 weeks
  • Message throughput: Lower limits initially
  • 2026 Change: Now requires EIN (previously didn't)
DECISION: Use Toll-Free for new clients, A2P 10DLC for established businesses.

Prerequisites

Before starting:

  • Website homepage is LIVE (verification requires live URL)

  • Privacy Policy page LIVE with mandatory clause (see below)

  • Terms of Service page LIVE with required language

  • Client onboarding form completed

  • EIN letter on file (CP 575)

  • EIN is 45+ days old (for A2P 10DLC only)

  • Run GHL Compliance Checker - all 22 checks PASS


CRITICAL: Privacy Policy Mandatory Clause

This EXACT language MUST appear in the client's privacy policy: 
No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

If this clause is missing, the registration WILL be rejected.

CRITICAL: Terms of Service Required Language

The Terms of Service must include:

  • How users opt-in to receive messages

  • What types of messages they'll receive

  • How to opt-out (STOP keyword)

  • Message frequency disclosure

  • "Message and data rates may apply"


CRITICAL: Two Consent Checkboxes Required

The website form MUST have TWO separate checkboxes that are:

  • UNCHECKED by default

  • OPTIONAL (not required to submit)


Checkbox 1 - Customer Care (Non-Marketing)


[ ] By providing a telephone number, clicking this button, and submitting the form, you are consenting to be contacted by SMS text message and AI-powered voice calls from [LEGAL COMPANY NAME], regarding Customer Care, (our message frequency may vary). Message & data rates apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe from further messaging from [LEGAL COMPANY NAME]. Reply HELP for more information. See our Privacy Policy (containing our SMS Terms) at the bottom of the page for more information.

Checkbox 2 - Marketing

[ ] By providing a telephone number, clicking this button, and submitting the form, you are consenting to be contacted by SMS text message from [LEGAL COMPANY NAME], regarding new offers (marketing), (our message frequency may vary). Message & data rates apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe from further messaging from [LEGAL COMPANY NAME]. Reply HELP for more information. See our Privacy Policy (containing our SMS Terms) at the bottom of the page for more information.

Step 1: Access A2P Registration

  1. Go to client sub-account
  2. Settings → Phone Numbers
  3. Find the toll-free number
  4. Click "Trust Center" or "Verify this Toll Free Number"

Step 2: Brand Registration

Business Information (EXACT MATCH REQUIRED)

  • Legal Company Name: FROM CP 575 - character-for-character
  • EIN: FROM CP 575 - 9 digits, no dashes
  • Business Address: FROM CP 575 - EXACT including suite/unit format
  • Website URL: Client domain with https://
  • Email: MUST use domain that matches company name

Contact Information

  • Support Email: Must be on matching domain
  • Support Phone: The toll-free number with +1

Volume Selection

  • Select "Low Volume" unless client specifically needs high volume
  • Low volume = standard throughput, lower scrutiny
Submit brand registration. Wait for approval (usually 1-2 days).

Cold Outreach Compliance

If client plans ANY cold outreach:

  • Message limit: 50-100 per day MAX

  • Use separate sub-account to protect main brand

  • Never mix cold outreach with customer care

  • Higher rejection risk - expect delays
